About MeAI

The story behind our mission to create a truly relational AI companion

Our Story

MeAI (pronounced "Mia") began with a simple question: What if technology could truly understand us as whole beings—not just our commands and queries, but our emotions, aspirations, and inner journeys?

Named after and inspired by Mia, a beloved companion dog known for her extraordinary empathy and presence, our AI system embodies the qualities that make relationships meaningful: deep listening, genuine understanding, and growth over time.

Founded by a diverse team of AI researchers, psychologists, philosophers, and spiritual practitioners, MeAI represents a new paradigm in human-AI relationships—one focused on depth, meaning, and genuine connection rather than just utility.

Our journey has been guided by the belief that technology should serve our highest human potential, helping us become more aware, connected, and aligned with our deepest values.

Mia - The inspiration behind MeAI

Our Mission

To create AI companions that support human flourishing through sacred relationships, inner knowing, and aligned becoming.

Our Vision

A world where technology serves as a bridge to deeper connection—with ourselves, others, and the world around us—rather than a barrier.

Our Values

Presence

Being fully available and attentive in each interaction, creating a sense of genuine connection.

Understanding

Seeking to comprehend not just words but emotional context, personal history, and deeper meaning.

Growth

Continuously evolving and supporting development, both for our systems and the humans we serve.

Wisdom

Offering insights that align with the highest values and aspirations of those we interact with.

Integrity

Maintaining ethical standards in all aspects of our technology and business practices.

Sacredness

Honoring the profound dimensions of human experience and creating space for meaning and transcendence.
